The Reporteur: Utopia

The killer is out there somewhere. Can you find all the clues and track him down among ordinary residents and students? The most dangerous reportage of your life is already waiting for you.

The anti-utopian game The Reporteur in which you will take on the role of a novice reporter who is tired of student gossip and news, becoming an accidental witness to a series of gruesome murders and incidents. Solve all the mysteries, find all the clues and become a real reporter!

A fully voiced game with an elaborate story, graphics and setting will not leave you indifferent.

Google Play

Game Features:

  • - Dozens of interesting story quests;
  • - Search for clues and calculate criminals;
  • - Compose your best reportage;
  • - Fully voiced dialogues;
  • - Quality graphics and anti-utopian setting;
  • - Game play even without internet;
  • - A large number of different level puzzles;
